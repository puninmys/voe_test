Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical installation take?

Most projects are completed within 2-3 weeks from consultation to final installation, depending on complexity and customization requirements.

Do you provide warranties?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties on all our installations. Pergolas and screens come with a 5-year structural warranty, and skylights include manufacturer warranties.

Can you work with existing structures?

Absolutely! We specialize in integrating our solutions with existing architecture and can adapt our designs to complement your current outdoor spaces.

What maintenance is required?

Our products are designed for minimal maintenance. We provide detailed care instructions and offer annual maintenance services to keep your installations in perfect condition.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

Yes, we assist with all necessary permits and approvals required for your project, ensuring compliance with local building codes and regulations.

Can I see examples of your work?

We'd be happy to show you our portfolio and can arrange visits to completed projects in your area. Contact us to schedule a consultation and site visit.
